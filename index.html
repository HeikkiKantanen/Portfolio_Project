About me

I'm a junior Full Stack Developer and I have vocational qualification in ICT. I studied in a fast track coding program designed for adult career changers in Business College Helsinki. The program lasted about 1,5 years and I graduated in December 2022. I have also previously graduated as a bachelor of automotive engineering from Metropolia School of Applied Sciences.

I'm enthusiastic about learning new things and I love to create web pages and applications. I like to keep the design clean and fresh and I'm also interested about UI design. I feel more at home with front end coding but I would like to get better in the back end as well. I also have experience of mobile application coding with React Native from my internship period.

I'm always happy to connect with new people and open for new interesting opportunities. So feel free to send me a message, email or connect in LinkedIn.
